Payment Method*

Make your cash payments at our Hopeful children's Ministries office along Kampala Road or Mpigi Road during office hours (Monday to Friday; 8:00 am to 5:00pm). Be sure to get a receipt for your cash payment.

Write your cheque out to Hopeful children's Ministries. Bring it to the Hopeful children's office along Kampala Road or Mpigi Road during office hours (Monday to Friday; 8:00 am to 5:00pm). Be sure to get a receipt for your cheque payment.

Our office will send you instructions and a form to fill out within the next 48 working hours to set up a Direct Debit with us.
