Sponsorship

Hopeful children's Ministries rescues vulnerable children who are a result of incest . What we desire is to give them a meaningful life by providing to them the basic needs of life.
Therefore when you do sponsor a child, you help us provide food, clothing, medical care, education. These children need sponsors in order to give them the holistic care so that they can have a good life like other children.

We call upon sponsors to partner with us and sponsor these children because we usually have new ones that come in for support. We would like you to take this opportunity and Check out the stories of some of our children. Please Sign up today and change a life today.
